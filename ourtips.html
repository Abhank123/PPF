What do we Offer?
Praeparatio.org's mission, is to improve the communication skills of students in India. Underneath, you will find certain competitions and exams that we offer tips for; The World Scholar's Cup, and Trinity College London. With these tips, we aim to help you exceed and improve your communication skills. Once you visit, you will find basic tips, information, and resources based on these events. You can also get detailed tips on the 'WSC' and 'Trinity' pages.
Enjoy!
