So who are we?
Prepcomm.in is a non-profit youth organization, whose mission is to improve the communication skills of 21st century citizens, and more particularly generation Z individuals in India. We strive to create a world, where conflicts, and misunderstandings can be resolved by dialogue, and where we aren't scared to raise our voices against the various injustices and problems facing the youth of today.
