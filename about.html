About
A little bit about us..
Who are we?
Prepcomm.in is a non-profit educational organization, run by enthusiastic high school students, whose goal is to improve the communication skills of students in India. Our team consists of students who have been debaters for more than three years and have participated in the World Scholar's Cup, and have given Trinity College London examinations. Our motive is to use the skills that we have developed from these events to help other students and encourage them to practice public speaking.
Why are we doing this?
After some thorough research, we discovered that a large population of Indian youth lack knowledge in communication skills, and this essentially prevents many from getting jobs. Communication skills are extremely important, and one cannot live without them. Therefore, we decided to create a website which would aim to help students develop communication skills.

Why a Website?

As a result of the COVID-19 Pandemic, in person learning was terminated and we felt that a website would be the next best option, as it can reach wide audiences.
How have we developed this website and its tips?
You may be wondering why this website looks so bad (not really), and it's because it has been coded from scratch, using Hyper-text Markup Language (HTML), Cascading Style Sheets (CSS), and JavaScript (JS). Our tips have been developed by qualified debaters and public speakers who have attended these very events.
